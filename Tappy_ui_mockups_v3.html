N · Rich Indigo

True deep indigo — not black, but a warm blue-violet dark. Cards are lighter indigo, the detail disc is amber (the original warm accent kept alive). Cozy and distinctive. Closest to "what if the current light-mode UI went dark but stayed warm."

At a glance: I is electric and gaming-adjacent. J is soft and glassy, very current. K is the lightest lift from where the app is today. L is the most dramatic and painterly. M is the quietest — purple as a single surgical accent. N keeps the warm amber from the existing UI but wraps it in indigo dark mode. Mix and match — the typography and layout are identical across all six, so individual decisions (background, active tile treatment, pill style) can be freely combined.
