Tappy Privacy Support

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Summary

Tappy is a macOS utility that plays local sound effects in response to keyboard activity across your Mac. Tappy does not record, sell, or transmit your typed text.

What Tappy accesses

Tappy requests macOS Input Monitoring permission so it can detect key events and trigger local sound playback while you type in other apps.

  • Tappy observes key timing and key codes to decide which local sound to play.
  • Tappy does not record or store the text you type as user content.
  • Tappy does not upload your typing to a remote server.
  • You can revoke Input Monitoring permission at any time in System Settings.

Data collection

Tappy does not require account creation and does not use a backend service for core functionality.

  • No account data is collected by Tappy itself.
  • No cloud sync is required for sound packs or settings.
  • Purchase processing for premium packs is handled by Apple through the App Store.

Local data

Tappy stores basic local preferences on your Mac, such as the currently selected sound pack and premium unlock state.

Third-party services

If you purchase premium content, Apple may process purchase-related data under Apple’s own policies. Review Apple’s App Store privacy information for those details.
